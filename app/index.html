Savusavu Fishing

Welcome to Savusavu Fishing with Transtar Charters.

Transtar Charters

Transtar Charters provide an exceptional sport and game fishing experience, operating out of the town of Savusavu in Fiji. Owned and operated by Terry Gray, who has been fishing out of Savusavu since 1995, Transtar Charters offer:

  • Sport and Game Fishing Charters
  • Island Transfers
  • Harbour Cruises

Depending on the conditions, you could catch Walu, Mahimahi, Wahoo, Yellowfin Tuna, or maybe even a Marlin. Visit the Savusavu Fishing blog to read about some of our trips.

Searov

SEAROV is a 35ft sport fishing game boat. It is fully rigged with Penn and Shimano 15 to 25kg tackle. You are welcome to bring your own favourite gear if you prefer.

The boat is fully marine surveyed with all safety gear, and is powered by two 200hp Volvo Penta inboard engines.

Savusavu

Savusavu is on the island of Vanua Levu in Fiji. The town is a short flight from Nadi or Suva, and boasts several wonderful resorts.

Fishing area

Transtar Charters offer fishing in an area from south of Savusavu to the island of Koro.
